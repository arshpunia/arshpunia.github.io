Tawa Paneer

Tawa is Hindi for skillet. This dish is more or less just sauteing some vegetables and the Paneer.

Ingredients

  • 400g Paneer - Diced into about 1-inch cubes
  • 1/2 White Onion - Sliced
  • 1 Green Pepper - Diced
  • 2 garlic cloves - chopped
  • Crushed tomatoes - 1 cup

Instructions

  • Heat olive oil over medium heat and add the onions and green peppers
  • Satue till the onions start to turn golden - About 5 minutes
  • Add the garlic - Saute for about 2 more minutes
  • Stir in the crushed tomatoes and rude heat by about 20% - Give the mexture about 5 more minutes
  • Bring the heat back up and add the Paneer. Stir well and often for about 15 minutes
  • Serve with pulao or naan/roti
